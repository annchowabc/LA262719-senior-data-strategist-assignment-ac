# Ethics Memo — to the Director

> One page maximum. Advise on the privacy and ethics questions this rollout raises,
> and on any request in the brief you would handle differently.

\# My advice to the Director on the privacy and ethics questions this rollout raises. 

1. The use of ai-copilot is a forward idea which needs to carefully assess the risk impact by including a human-in-the-loop checkpoint for review and agreement or revision.
2. Use human instinct and common sense during review of ai-copilot usage. 
3. For Confidential labels require a human review. For Restricted labels perhaps these should only be handled by humans. 
4. Carefully review the ai-copilot labelling suggestions with the departments involved to verify that they meet the criteria. Use a phased rollout starting with Public and Internal content only. 
5. Implement zero-retention mode for all non-Public content.
